Created by 3 dedicated university and industrial placement student at Web Applications UK. This app draws on 2 API service to produce a never before seen service where you can find out the likelihood of you being involved in a road accident when going to your favourite places. Our service allows you to choose 2 desired locations, listed from Yelp, using this information we combine this with road accident statistics through CDL's elastic API service to provide you with a comprehensive percentage rating of how likely to you are to be involved.

The story began 6pm on Friday 4th March 2016 at the Student Hack at Manchester Metropolitan Business School. After many gruelling hours of debate one idea stood above any other and thus Will I Make It was born, now at only less than 2 days old stands before you.
